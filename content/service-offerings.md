## Service Model Overview

Stones and Stacks offers consulting and implementation services across four interconnected practice areas. Engagements range from strategic advisory (1–4 week assessments) to full implementation (8–16 week build-and-deploy projects).

**Engagement Model Options:**
- **Assessment & Roadmap** — Audit current state, produce strategy + execution plan (2–4 weeks)
- **Implementation** — Build, deploy, and hand off (6–16 weeks)
- **Retainer** — Ongoing advisory, tuning, and support (monthly)
- **Workshop / Training** — Upskilling teams (1–3 day sessions)
